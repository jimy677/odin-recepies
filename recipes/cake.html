Cake

It's a cake with many Ingredients that makes the cake soo sweet

Ingredients

  • 2 cups crushed fresh strawberries.
  • 1(6 ounce) package strawberry-flavored gelatin.
  • 3 cups miniature marshmallows.
  • 1(15.25) package yellow cake mix.
  • 1 cup water.
  • 3 large eggs.
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil.

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Spread the crushed strawberries on the bottom of a 9*13-inch banking dish;sprinkle gelatin powder over strawberries,then top with th mini marshmallows.
  3. Combine cake mix,water,eggs,and oil in a large bowl;beat with an electic mixer on low speed untill moistened.Increase speed to medium and beat for 2 minutes.Pour battter on top of marshmallows.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean,40 to 50 minutes
  5. Cool cake in the pan for 15 minutes.Run a knife around the edges,the turn out the cake onto a serving plate,strawberry side up.Store cake in the refigerator
